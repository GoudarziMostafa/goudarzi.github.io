Hello! I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Finance & Business Analytics Group and Ramp-Up Manager at Viipuri Lab, LUT Business School, Finland. My current research focuses on understanding trade behavior in sequential trading with a specific interest in herd behavior. I aim to replicate this behavior in an AI trader and study the trade dynamics in a system that includes this AI trader, using a complex systems approach.

For more information, please check my CV or do not hesitate to contact me via email at mostafa.goudarzi@lut.fi.
